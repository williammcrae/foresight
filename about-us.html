About Us

"Practical, reliable and efficient engineering solutions"

Welcome to Foresight Engineering Services. We aim to provide a professional, efficient and competitive resource for all of your engineering needs.

Foresight was established in 2009 through the amalgamation of a number of contracting professional engineers working together under a single company structure.

Our focus is providing quality engineering solutions to both local government and private sector clients on time and on budget.

We utilise the latest CAD software and survey equipment, and ensure that all personnel are fully briefed on legislation affecting the industry, current design trends and the latest innovations in the field.
