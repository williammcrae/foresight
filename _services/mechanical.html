  • Bulk materials handling equipment
  • Hazardous and Dangerous Goods storage and handling facilities.
  • Machinery safety systems
  • Access platforms compliance and integrity
  • Hazardous Goods storage and handling
  • Process troubleshooting and identification of bottlenecks
  • Facilitation of risk assessments with client representatives
  • Hydraulic equipment and associated pipework
  • Mechanical components and assemblies
  • Design and certification of lifting equipment
